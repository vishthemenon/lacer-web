About The Lacer System

The design consists of 3 main components - ealed inflatable polypropylene air pouch that is lined by flocked PVC, handheld air pump and optional pump mount.

  • The air pump can be easily attached to the pouch hands-free
  • With a button or the app (coming soon), the air pouch gets inflated
  • Once inflated, the pouch exerts pressure against the tongue of the shoe and foot

Custom pneumatic, inflation mechanism

Our inflatable pouches are designed to go under the tongue of your shoes and can be designed to wrap around your feet or sit on top of your toes

  • Light, easy-to-install with Velcro straps
  • Comes with a flocked PVC outer lining for extra comfort
  • Able to stay inflated for extended periods
