How it Works

The Lacer system comes with a custom-designed inflatable pouch that slips under the tongue of your shoe and wrap around your feet. Once inflated remotely, it keeps your feet snug within your shoe.

Making life a little easier

Though a seemingly mundane task, tying shoelaces can be a difficult task for the elderly and people with injuries or diseases affecting fine motor skills

  • Tie your laces without straining your back
  • Simplify the intricacies of tying your laces
  • Never trip over undone laces

Pick you style

Lacer fits on most shoes easily so you are free to show of your style without compromising on convienience. Its innovative design also enables you to remove it easily and use it with different pairs of shoes.

  • Light, compact and unnoticable
  • Does not use adhesives or permenant stitching
  • Can be removed and replaced easily
